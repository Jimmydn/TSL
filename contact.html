Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries? +

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent matters, please call our customer support line.

I'm having technical issues with the website. Who should I contact? +

For technical support, please email tech@thespicylist.com with details about the issue you're experiencing. Screenshots are always helpful!

How do I report inappropriate content? +

You can report inappropriate content by clicking the "Report" button on any profile or by contacting our moderation team at moderation@thespicylist.com.

Do you offer affiliate or partnership opportunities? +

Yes! We're always looking for strategic partners. Please contact us at partnerships@thespicylist.com with your proposal.
